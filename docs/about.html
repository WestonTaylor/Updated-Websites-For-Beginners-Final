The background of CSA

Compassion for Sheltered Animals (a.k.a CSA) is a high school club at Exeter High School. CSA was first established in 2013. CSA was founded by Exeter alumni Gloria Torres (above) (left), and has been managed throughout the years by Brenda Camren (second picture below) (right) in order to connect animals in shelters with a loving home. Over the last 8 years CSA has been able to work with many community members to accomplish this goal, and every member looks forward to the future where we can once again connect with one another in a safe manner.
